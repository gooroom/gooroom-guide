Dockbar - Panel Area

Panel area, located on the left side of the Dockbar, shows the launcher menu
which contains apps provided by Gooroom OS and status of running tasks.

You can easily access frequently used apps or jobs by adding them to the panel.

Dockbar - Tray Area

Tray area, located on the right side of the Dockbar, shows system preferences of Gooroom Platform.
 It contains update manager, keyboard settings, network settings, sound,
security status, date and time, system menu settings.
